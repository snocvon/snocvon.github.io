About Me.

I am Von Ivan Lopez, a designer, photographer, and developer currently pursuing a Bachelor of Science in Information Technology as a second-year student at Datamex College of Saint Adeline. I began developing my design skills in junior high school and have largely refined them through self-directed learning and hands-on practice.

Driven by a strong passion for the arts, I blend creativity with technical problem-solving across visual design, photography, and software development. My work focuses on translating ideas into functional and visually compelling digital experiences, with a growing interest in building solutions that are both aesthetic and practical.
